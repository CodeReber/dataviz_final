Arctic Circle is home to Polar bears.

Polar bears are only found in the Arctic primarily in arctic sea ice, water, islands, and continental coastlines. Polar bears undergo seasonal migrations that is largely dependent on movements of sea ice packs. Polar bears can travel up to 19 miles or more per day for several days with a typical home range of about 20,000 sq.miles.
